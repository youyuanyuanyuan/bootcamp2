    • Self-made Hairpins

    In the process of exploring traditional Chinese costume and hairstyles, I was inspired by these delicate, gorgeous hair decorations. So I began to try to make these these hairpins by myself. First of all, I brought a bunch of metal and glass component with an instruction set. Then I sketch of  what I hope this hairpin would  looks like. Finally  assembled necessary components together to make an  functional hairpin according to my sketch.
