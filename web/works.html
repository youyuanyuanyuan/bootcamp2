    • Posters made for NUCSSA

    NUCSSA is the abbreviation for Chinese Scholars and Student's Association. I am currently the director of Designing department which formed by 10 people. As a director, my daily job is to allocate designing tasks to members. Design department is an essential department to NUCSSA, posters and videos we produced are the principal way to get people's attention to our activities and to NUCSSA.
